---
title: "Branding"
description: "Make the client portal yours — name, logo, and accent color."
---

The client portal is your agency's front door, so it wears your brand, not
Relay's. Set it once from the desktop **Branding** page.

## What you can set

- **Agency name** — the portal title and sign-in heading.
- **Logo** — an image up to **1 MB**, stored inline as a data URI (or an
  `https` URL). No object storage required.
- **Accent color** — a `#rrggbb` hex. The portal themes its primary color from
  it, computing readable contrast for text automatically.

## Where it shows

Branding is served publicly at `GET /portal/branding` — the login page needs
it before a client authenticates — so your name and logo appear on:

- the portal sign-in page,
- the portal header once signed in,
- the team invite join page.

<Note>
  The "Powered by Relay" badge stays on the portal. It renders the Relay mark
  and links back — the portal is your brand, attributed to the platform.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Keep the logo well under 1 MB — a tight PNG or SVG-as-data-URI loads fast and
  looks crisp on the portal header at small sizes.
</Tip>
